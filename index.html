Introduction

Welcome to Mubarak Goods Forwarding Agency, your trusted partner for reliable truck transport services. With a fleet of modern vehicles and experienced professionals, we ensure the safe and timely transportation of goods across Pakistan.

Key Features

  • Fleet Variety: Modern trucks (container, flatbed, refrigerated) for diverse cargo needs.
  • Experienced Drivers: Highly trained, licensed, and background-checked drivers.
  • Real-Time Tracking: GPS-enabled vehicles for monitoring and updates.
  • Safety First: Secure cargo handling, insurance coverage, and adherence to regulations.
  • Flexibility: Tailored solutions for varied cargo sizes, weights, and types.
  • Timely Deliveries: Guaranteed on-time pickups and deliveries.
  • Competitive Pricing: Cost-effective solutions without compromising quality.
  • Customer Support: Dedicated team for assistance and queries.

Services Offered

  • Full Truck Load (FTL)
  • Less Than Truck Load (LTL)
  • Inter-State and Intra-State Transport
  • Refrigerated Transport
  • Oversized Cargo Transport
  • Door-to-Door Delivery
  • Warehousing and Storage
